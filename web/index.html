Why portal
To help to achieve new goals at higher efficiency

CSR stands for Commonn Social Responsibility.According to CSR, every coorporates and the private sectors have the responsibility towards the welfare of the society. In this modern era, these private sectors and corporates are working to make life easier and technological advanced, but they do much harm to the environment in the form of pollution and wastes coming from the companies of these private sectors and corporates. Basically, according to the law, every company has to compulsariliy spend their 2% of the revenue towards social developmet and social events.

Common CSR Portal
Lets work together foe the betterment of the society

A common portal for individual/corporate/organization will help in proper execution of any CSR program. This portal will allow anyone to create their own event. For successful accomplishment of the event they can describe their needs on the portal. Along with the needs other details will also be displayed in the portal. If event requires funding or manpower, various corporate can join hands together to accomplish the task or event. Even individuals, who want to contribute their time/money for a social event can contact the host organization.
